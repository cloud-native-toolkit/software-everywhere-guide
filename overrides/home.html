Software Everywhere

What is Software Everywhere
To help the adoption of IBM Hybrid Cloud Software through our Ecosystem, we have launched Software Everywhere initiative. The programme is designed to deliver a set of Site Reliability Engineering automation. The automation would help IBM Partners to accelerate adoption of IBM Technology on key Hyperscaler partnerships, specifically AWS and Azure and IBM Cloud.
Target Personas
The SWE SRE assets and tools are targeted to cover three personas:
  • Tech Sellers--Leads Technical Sales of IBM Technology combined with Partner Offerings
  • Prototype Build Engineer--Development team member focused on designing prototype
  • Production SRE--SRE skilled who uses software and automation to solve problems
